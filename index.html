FAQS

Frequently asked questions? You really are curious. I'll start with the biggest one, "what is copywriting?" Copywriting is the text/ideas behind marketing, sales, ads, and other promotional material. It is the use of words to get a reader to take action. There are two types of copy, short-form, and long-form (small vs. big projects.) If those words were too big for you, check my recent work for some examples

FAQ #2: Why do I want a copywriter? What do they do? I'll answer both at once. A copywriter is someone advanced in marketing who writes the persuasive text. The end goal is for the reader to take action. Businesses want copywriters to ensure high conversion rates on their ads/promotions.

FAQ #3: How much does copywriting cost? The answer everyone loves to hear; It depends. The price goes up and down based on the size of the project, the company, and your copywriter's skills. Typically, the pricing is mentioned while blueprinting a project with your copywriter.

FAQ #4: Should I work with you on my copywriting? Probably not. In some cases, You may already have too much website traffic! If that's you, I'm sorry to hear that; I tend to bring in more :(. Your product may be unethical, and I don't want to help promote it. Perhaps, you hate dealing with customers in general! If any of that applies to you, click off this website and go about your merry day. Now, say none of that applies to you; you may be cool enough to email my personal.
